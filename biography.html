Biography

Journey of an Artist

Adrian Buba's artistic journey began in the heart of Transylvania, where the dramatic landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Romania would forever shape his creative vision. Born on June 8, 1953, in Caransebeș, Buba emerged as a singular voice in contemporary art, masterfully navigating the space between abstraction and figuration.

"Adrian Buba reconciles a certain figurative appearance with the free benefits of abstract art." — René Huyghe, Art Historian

After graduating at the top of his class from the National University of Art in Bucharest in 1976, Buba spent four formative years as a conservator at the National Art Museum of Romania. This intimate engagement with the masters of Romanian art provided him with a deep understanding of artistic tradition while nurturing his desire to forge a unique contemporary path.

The pivotal moment came in 1980 when Buba left Romania for Germany, eventually settling in Paris in 1981. This transition marked not just a geographical shift but an artistic metamorphosis. In Paris, the epicenter of modern art, Buba found the freedom to fully explore his distinctive style—one that would earn him international recognition and placement in prestigious collections worldwide.

Career Timeline

1953

Birth in Transylvania

Born June 8 in Caransebeș, Romania, in the heart of Transylvania's dramatic landscape.

1976

Academic Excellence

Graduated top of class from the National University of Art in Bucharest. Began work as conservator at the National Art Museum of Romania.

1979

National Recognition

Received the prestigious Romanian National Painting Prize, establishing himself as a leading figure in Romanian contemporary art.

1981

Paris Migration

Settled in Paris after leaving Romania via Germany. Co-founded the artistic collective "Die Gruppe".

1982

Cité Internationale des Arts

Major exhibition at the prestigious Cité internationale des arts in Paris, marking his arrival on the international art scene.

1983

Prix d'Art Sacré

Awarded the Prix d'Art Sacré at the Monte-Carlo International Contemporary Art Prize.

1984

French Citizenship

Became a French citizen, solidifying his position in the Parisian art world.

2014

Asian Expansion

Exhibition at Ningbo Museum of Art in China, marking significant expansion into Asian art markets.
